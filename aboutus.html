What is TransitAura?

At TransitAura, our mission is to make public transit safer and more accessible for everyone. By providing real-time safety insights and easy access to emergency contacts, we aim to empower transit users to make informed choices.

Who are we?

We’re a team of first-term Computer Systems Technology students with a passion for making a difference. With fresh perspectives and a shared dedication to public safety, we’ve come together to create an app that helps transit users stay informed and safe.

Why did we build this app?

Every transit user deserves peace of mind. We built this app to help communities stay informed about potential safety issues, creating a safer transit environment for everyone.
